SNU -- Schools Need U!

Matching volunteers with schools that need their help.

With SNU, you can:

  • Match your availability to opportunities at a school near you
  • Pick based on your special skills, or just a desire to help
  • Track your hours and earn points and kudos
  • Earn kudos faster for volunteering at those schools that need help most
  • Automatically submit for corporate matching at participating companies
  • Share your enthusiasm for volunteering
  • Let your friends know how to get involved too
